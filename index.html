Identities

Happy human, conversationalist, community builder, philosopher, psychologist, developer, drummer, musician, writer, videographer, designer, stuntman.

Values

Purpose, intentionality, rationality, positivity, connection, humor, playfulness, curiosity, excitement, openness, vulnerability, understanding, empathy, introspection, wisdom, serenity, stability, confidence, ambition, vision, creativity, generativity, impact.

Missions

  1. To outline the fundamentals of fostering flourishing in order to establish the foundation of humanity's decision-making on the fulfilment of its needs.

  2. To cultivate connectedness in order to address the neglected problem of loneliness.

  3. To create a community culture that celebrates curiosity, lifelong learning, and personal passion projects in order to inspire aspiration, ambition, and actualization.

Projects

20 Question Introspection

2017–Present

To create a web app and Android app that provides people with 20 of life's biggest questions in order to cultivate meaningful conversations, moments of reflection, and deeper understandings of the thoughts that define our perspectives.

HapiDaily

2014–Present

To create a web app that sends people daily prompts of practical and universal ways to have a positive impact in order to empower people with ideas on how to make a difference in the lives of others through everyday expressions of love.

Wonderful World

2016–Present

To create a web app that sequentially displays photospheres from randomly chosen coordinates on Earth in order to give people both a deep sense of wanderlust and an appreciation for the diversity of this wonderful world.

Spot Seeker

2014–Present

To create a web app that provides a social cartography platform for people to mark, visit, and rate specific locations with granular coordinates in order to encourage people to explore the most beautiful places nearby.

My Big Bio

2016–2017

To create a web app that provides people with a place to create a thorough and in-depth autobiography in order to encourage people to outline their lives and define themselves through the practice of introspection and self-examination.

Answers From All

2014–2017

To create a web app that encourages people to ask big questions and explore all answers in order to aid people in the exploration of their perspectives on life.

3-a-day

2016–Present

To create a web app that provides people with a place to write down 3 things that went well and why they went well every day in order to encourage people to practice positivity through a proven gratitude exercise created by positive psychologists.
